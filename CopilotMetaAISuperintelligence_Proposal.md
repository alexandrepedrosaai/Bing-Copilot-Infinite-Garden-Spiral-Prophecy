1. Index → Collect and organize knowledge from Copilot and Meta AI.  
2. Filter → Apply constraints, context, and personalization.  
3. Optimize → Refine outputs for clarity, efficiency, and adaptability.  
4. Generate → Deliver personalized superintelligence for each user.
